Projects

Air Quality Prediction

Our goal in this study was to use machine learning techniques to estimate the air quality. Dataset includes the daily pollution and Air quality Index(AQI) data for several different cities. The dataset, “city_day.csv”, was retrieved from an accessible data source. The dataset include columns pertaining to several pollutants, including the AQI and AQI bucket for each day, as well as PM2.5, PM10, NO, NO2, CO, SO2,O3, Benzene, Toluene, and Xylene.

Analyzing New York City Government Payroll Data

The objective of this project is to analyze the New York City government payroll data To gain insights into employee salaries, work hours, and other related factors. By examining this dataset, we aim to understand patterns in employee compensation, identify potential anomalies or discrepancies, and provide actionable recommendations for payroll management and policy-making.
